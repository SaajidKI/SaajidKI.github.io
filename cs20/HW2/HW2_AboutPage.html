About Us

Jurassic Snuggles is a small business based in Toronto, Canada that was founded in 2022. Our mission is to bring joy and comfort to people of all ages with our adorable and cuddly dinosaur plushies. Our team is dedicated to creating high-quality products that are made with care and attention to detail.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring happiness to everyone who loves dinosaurs and plushies by creating soft, cuddly, and high-quality products. We believe that everyone deserves to have a friend to snuggle with, and we are committed to making that happen.
